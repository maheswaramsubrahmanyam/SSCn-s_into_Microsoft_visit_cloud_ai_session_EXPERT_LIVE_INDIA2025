Our Travel Experience

Starting Point

I, along with Sai Akash, began our trip from Machilipatnam. We were later joined by Gayathri, Sreelakshmi, Yashwanth, Dileep, and Madhan at Gudivada Station.

Train Delay

Our train to Secunderabad was delayed by two hours, so we reached at 7:45 AM and took the metro.

Ticket Mishap

I mistakenly bought metro tickets for only 7 people, leaving Sreelakshmi without a ticket. However, we managed the situation quickly and had a fun time navigating the metro.

Crazy Auto Ride

One crazy moment was squeezing 8 people into a single auto to reach Microsoft Gachibowli. It was chaotic but incredibly fun!

Hurdles at Microsoft

Upon reaching Gate 4 at Microsoft Office, some challenges awaited us:

  • Sreelakshmi's Missing Confirmation: She didn’t have the confirmation ticket, but I managed to get her inside.
  • Nandyal Team's Delay: The mentors and students from Nandyal arrived late and weren’t allowed in. After much effort and with Swathi’s mother’s support, 5 members, including 4 SSC mentors, were able to attend.

Special thanks to Swathi’s mother, whose determination made all the difference. I even gifted her one of the medals I received for answering Q&A sessions during the event – a small token of respect for her efforts.

Key Highlights of the Event

  • Networking: I connected with industry leaders like Morten Knudsen, Praveen Singampalli, and Kamal Shree. The SSC Community was widely recognized, and many participants appreciated our work.
  • Recognition: I answered multiple questions during the sessions, earning two medals and a t-shirt.
  • Memorable Lunch: We had a delicious lunch and clicked team photos to capture the day’s memories.

Why Attend Events Like This?

After the event, we held a mentors’ meeting to discuss the key takeaways:

  • These events shift mindsets, inspire innovation, and open up opportunities for internships, jobs, and networking.
  • They help build communities and connections that can shape our future careers.

The Journey Back: A Crazy Adventure

After bidding goodbye to Microsoft, we faced another challenge while traveling back. Our plans to book a bus failed, so we took a general train. Despite being tired, the journey was filled with laughter, learning, and small ego fights.

Ending on a High Note

One of the best moments was a surprise birthday gift from SSC mentors. It truly made my day and filled me with gratitude.

This experience has strengthened my desire to work at Microsoft someday and made me even more committed to expanding the Student Service Community.

Thank you to the Microsoft team, SSC mentors, and everyone who made this day so special.
